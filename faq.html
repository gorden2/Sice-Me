ANSWERS

  • How does this work?

    • It’s simple. You place your order, the restaurant will make it and then we pick it up and deliver it to you.

  • How fast do you deliver?

    • Most orders are delivered in approximately 45 minutes. During busier hours, it might take up to an hour.

  • What is the delivery fee?

    • The delivery fee is $5. The best way is to split it between your friends.

  • Should I tip the driver?

    • How nice of you to ask! While it is not required, our delivers would appreciate it.

  • Can I order in advance?

    • Of course – you could place your order up to 2 days in advance.

  • What is Sice Me’s refund policy?

    • We want to delight you. If you ever encounter an issue, please email us at contactus@Siceme.com or call us at (phone).

  • Are your menu prices the same as those at the restaurant?

    • The prices you pay at Sice Me are the same as you would pay at the restaurants.

  • Can I order from different restaurants at the same time?

    • Currently, you cannot. However, we are working on it.

  • Where do you deliver?

    • We deliver within a 1.5-mile radius of University of Maryland.

  • What are your hours of operation?

    • We are open Monday through Friday from 5 p.m. to 9:30p.m.

  • What are Sice Me points?

    • For every order you place, you would get points. Depending on the amount of points, you would get a percentage off your next order.
